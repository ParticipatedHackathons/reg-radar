The Problem
When RBI releases a new regulation circular, banks have no automated way to determine what it impacts. Compliance officers manually read through it and bounce between teams, systems, and documents to figure out what needs to change. This is slow, error-prone, and high-stakes.
The Solution
A multi-agent system where a compliance officer uploads an RBI circular and gets back a structured impact report — which banking applications, business processes, compliance controls, and engineering teams need to change, with priorities and deadlines.

Agent Pipeline
AgentResponsibilityIngestion AgentParses the RBI circular PDF, extracts key directives and deadlinesRegulation Mapping AgentMaps directives to banking functions (KYC, AML, payments etc.)Application Impact AgentIdentifies which internal systems are affectedProcess Impact AgentIdentifies which business processes need updatingCompliance Control AgentChecks which existing controls need revision or creationTeam Routing AgentAssigns action items to teams with priorities and deadlinesReport AgentSynthesizes everything into a structured impact brief
Agents 2–6 run inside the Band coordination layer. Agents 1 and 7 are entry and exit points.
